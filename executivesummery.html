Mayondo Wood & Furniture Ltd

High-Level Business Report & Strategic Recommendations

Executive Summary

In July 2025, Mayondo Wood & Furniture (MWF) recorded strong performance in both the wood and furniture categories. Sales agents drove significant growth, with top contributions from institutional clients such as construction companies and estates. Transport services, which attract a 5% surcharge, were applied in ~45% of transactions, adding a notable revenue stream.
